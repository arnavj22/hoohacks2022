Features




Pushup Tracking

We track joint movement in both the arms and back to ensure a student is bending their arms 90 degrees while their back is flat.

Planks

We use pose estimation to continuously monitor back posture and notify students when improper form sets in due to strain.

Curl Ups

We employ PoseNet to monitor the relation between wrist and knees to identify whether legs are bent at the proper position and if a student is going up at the proper angle.

Bicep Curls

We track hand movements such that dumbbells are coming to the shoulder while analyzing shoulder movement to inform students about swaying.

Squats

We perform pose estimation to find the relation knees and heels for proper form, hips to ensure students are going down all the way, and back posture to determine whether the curvature is straight.
