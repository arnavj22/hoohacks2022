What is TeleTrainer?

TeleTrainer utilizes state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning techniques to accurately provide real-time insights to better improve workout habits. In the age of online learning when students are cooped up behind cameras and where physical education classes now require students sending time-consuming videos of exercises without immediate feedback, TeleTrainer is a reliable solution allows students to learn and stay healthy while also allowing for direct and quick teacher feedback.

Developed by Arnav Jain, Sameer Gabbita, Sauman Das, Vishal Kotha
